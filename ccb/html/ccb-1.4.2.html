Requesting a Dhamma Talk

After bowing three times, with hands joined in añjali, recite the following:
Brahmā ca꜕ lokādhipa꜕tī sa꜕hampa꜕ti Ka꜕tañja꜕lī a꜕nadhiva꜕raṃ a꜕yāca꜕tha Santī꜓dha sa꜕ttāppa꜕ra꜕jakkha꜕-jātikā Desetu꜕ dhammaṃ a꜕nu꜕kampi꜕maṃ pa꜕jaṃ
Bow three times again
The Brahmā god Sahampati, Lord of the world, With palms joined in reverence, requested a favour: ‘Beings are here with but little dust in their eyes, Pray, teach the Dhamma out of compassion for them.’
